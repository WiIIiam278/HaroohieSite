Introduction

This guide will walk you through how to setup The Series of Haruhi Suzumiya English Fan Translation by dumping a copy of the game to a .nds ROM file, patching it using our .xdelta patch file and then playing it on your Nintendo DS or Nintendo 3DS system, or an emulator.

Prerequisites

You will need the following to play:

  • A copy of Suzumiya Haruhi no Chokuretsu for Nintendo DS
  • A Nintendo 3DS, 2DS, DSi or DS game system
  • A Windows, macOS or Linux computer
  • Any hardware-specific requirements needed to dump or play the game ROM depending on the device you'd like to use (e.g. a SD card or R4 card)
